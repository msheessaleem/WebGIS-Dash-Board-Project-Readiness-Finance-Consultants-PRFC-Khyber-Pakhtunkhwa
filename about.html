About Us

Who We Are

Project Readiness & Finance Consultants (PRFC), Khyber Pakhtunkhwa is a cutting-edge engineering and geospatial intelligence firm delivering sustainable, tech-driven infrastructure solutions.

Our Vision

Revolutionizing Pakistan's infrastructure and environmental planning by merging engineering excellence with digital geospatial innovation.

What We Do

  • GIS: Real-time dashboards, spatial databases, and web GIS for infrastructure.
  • Remote Sensing: Satellite/drone analytics for hydrology, snow melt, land use planning.
  • Civil Engineering: Smart irrigation, canal systems, water supply, and rural infrastructure.
  • AI + IoT Integration: Smart sensors for infrastructure monitoring and forecasting.

Innovation & Research

Collaborating with universities, global agencies, and leveraging ArcGIS Pro, GEE, Python, QGIS, and cloud GIS to create smart, data-driven solutions.
